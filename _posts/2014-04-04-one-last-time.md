title: 'One Last Time'
date: '2014-04-04 00:30:00'
---

<small>
i opened a wound in the earth  
to tuck you away  

and as the soil filled around you  
covering the last of my dreams  

i asked the roots  
and the rocks  
and the oceans  
and the sea  
to collapse around me  

so i could lay by your side  
*one last time.*
</small>
